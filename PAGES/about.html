Who We Are

Dandora Youth Community is a grassroots initative dedicated to empowering the youth through mental health education, support, and connection to services.

Our Vision:

A mentally healthy and supportive Dandora, where everyone feels heard, understood, and cared for

Our Mission:

To break the stigma around mental health, promote emotional well-being, and make mental health resources accessible to all.

Our Approch:

> Local engagement and peer support.

> Partnerships with professionals and clinics.

> Culturally relevant education and outreach.
